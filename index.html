Braille to SVG


Enter text to translate it to Braille and get a SVG representation. You can also opt to enter Braille directly if you already have a (better) translation. Press the download button to get a SVG representation. If the text is too big it won't show up on the screen but it will still be in the downloaded SVG.

Made by Zwettekop
